    Why this matters

    Monsoon-driven turbidity and contamination events can rapidly increase risk of diarrhoeal outbreaks. Rapid community reporting + low-cost sensors can cut detection & response time from days to hours.

    How it works

    1. Water sensors + gateway stream hourly readings.
    2. ASHAs submit syndromic reports (offline if needed).
    3. Edge rules + cloud scoring raise alerts and push SMS/IVR.
    4. Local SOPs guide investigation and interventions.

    Pilot deliverables

    • ASHA app & SMS gateway
    • 10 sensor kits + gateway
    • Dashboard + alerting rules
